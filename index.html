Our Philosophy

Healing the Fragility of the Wild

Surrounded by the rich ecological tapestry of the Western Ghats—one of the world’s most important biodiversity hotspots—Native Ground creates a living repository of functionally significant plant species.


We are transitioning from a conventional cardamom, coffee, and pepper estate into a rare sanctuary of biodiversity. Our goal is to promote peaceful coexistence between humans and wildlife through restorative habitats and ancient wisdom.

Eco-DRR

Biological Rebar: Engineering Resilience

Located strategically adjacent to the 2024 landslide zone, Native Ground serves as a model for Ecosystem-based Disaster Risk Reduction (Eco-DRR).


We secure the earth by planting deep-rooted native species and Vetiver grass, which acts as "biological soil nails" with a tensile strength of 85.1 MPa, stabilizing slopes and preventing future disasters for generations to come.

"We heal the land by planting life that secures the earth. It is a fusion of botanical science and geological engineering."

Slope Stabilization

Harnessing the power of nature to anchor the steep terrain of Wayanad without the use of concrete or invasive steel mesh.

Water Management

Restoring natural water runoff channels to maintain the hydrological balance of the fragile montane rainforest ecosystem.

Responsible Hospitality
