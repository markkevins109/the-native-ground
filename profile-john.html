Legal Advisor, Native Ground

Mr. M C John

Sr. Advocate, Hon. High Court, Kerala

Mr. John obtained his LLB from Government Law College, Ernakulam. He enrolled as an Advocate in the High Court of Kerala in 1973 and continues his practice to this day.

During his distinguished career, he was appointed as a Government Pleader in 1983. After three years, he was promoted to Senior Government Pleader, a position he held until 1993. During this period, he was entrusted with significant cases under the Forest Act, Arbitration Act, and Abkari Act. Following this, he continued his practice as a lawyer specializing in Civil and Constitutional matters.

On completion of 50 years of practice, he was honored with a 'Memento' presented by the Hon’ble Chief Justice of India, Mr. Justice D.Y. Chandrachud.

Beyond his legal practice, Mr. John has had the privilege of visiting no less than 32 countries abroad.
