Native Ground Founder’s Circle

A Community Committed to Restoring Nature

The Native Ground Founders Circle is a growing elite community of environmentally conscious individuals who believe that protecting and restoring the biodiversity of the Western Ghats is one of the most meaningful responsibilities of our time.

The Circle has been created to support the long-term ecological mission of Native Ground — to develop a biodiversity-rich, pesticide-free, and economically sustainable model of cardamom cultivation that works in harmony with nature.

Why the Founders Circle Matters

The forests and ecosystems of the Western Ghats are under increasing pressure from chemical-intensive agriculture, soil degradation, loss of biodiversity, and declining water systems.

Restoring these fragile ecosystems requires:

  • Long-term commitment
  • Scientific ecological restoration and Sustainable cultivation practices
  • Financial stability and public participation

The Founders Circle brings together individuals and institutions who wish to actively support this restoration journey and help create a living model of regenerative agriculture for the future.

What Your Support Helps Create

Support received through the Founders Circle will directly contribute towards:

Ecological Restoration

Restoring degraded land, rebuilding healthy soil, and reviving native vegetation.

Biodiversity Protection

Protecting pollinators, birds, insects, microorganisms, and fragile forest ecosystems.

Pesticide-Free Cultivation

Expanding environmentally responsible cardamom cultivation that reduces chemical dependency.

Water & Soil Conservation

Improving soil health, moisture retention, and long-term ecological resilience.

Protection of Vulnerable Land

Acquiring and safeguarding adjoining land threatened by chemical-intensive farming practices.

Research & Demonstration

Building a long-term model for sustainable and profitable cultivation integrated with biodiversity conservation.

Membership Categories

  • Patrons: Individuals, families, or institutions providing significant support towards ecological restoration, land protection, and long-term sustainability initiatives.
  • Founder Members: Early supporters helping establish the vision, foundation, and future direction of Native Ground.
  • Members: Nature-conscious individuals contributing towards biodiversity restoration and regenerative agriculture.
  • Student & Volunteer Supporters: Students, researchers, young changemakers, and volunteers interested in ecology, conservation, and sustainable farming.

What Members Become Part Of

Members of the Founders Circle become participants in a meaningful long-term environmental mission. They receive:

  • Recognition as supporters of Native Ground in ecological restoration
  • Certificate / Mementos / Special mention in the website and other collaterals
  • Priority access to Native Ground products and services with a special discount. Opportunities to interact with experts in ecology, soil science, biodiversity, and regenerative agriculture
  • Opportunities to observe and learn from a living model of sustainable cultivation in the Western Ghats

More Than Membership

The Native Ground Founders Circle is not simply a support network.

It is a collective effort to restore damaged ecosystems, protect biodiversity, preserve the ecological wealth of the Western Ghats, and help create a healthier future for generations to come.
