Director, Native Ground

George Cheriyan

Mr. George Cheriyan is a Mechanical Engineer with a Master’s degree in Production Engineering, bringing over four decades of diverse experience in engineering, Project execution, and project Management. Over the course of his career, he has successfully led and delivered complex initiatives across industries, combining technical depth with strategic vision.

As the Founder and CEO of The Aviation Space Network and Machinist.in, Mr. Cheriyan has built influential platforms that connect professionals across the global manufacturing and aerospace ecosystem. His extensive international exposure—including engagement with leading companies across developed nations has given him deep insight into global best practices and emerging industry trends.

At Native Ground, he brings not only his professional expertise and global network, but also a personal connection to the land. As a representative of the cardamom estate owners, he has been closely associated with the evolution and management of the estate from its early years. Deeply sensitive to nature and sustainability, Native Ground reflects his long-held vision of restoring ecological balance while creating a model for responsible land stewardship.

He is supported by a wide network of professionals and like-minded individuals across disciplines, united by a shared commitment to biodiversity, sustainability, and regenerative practices.
