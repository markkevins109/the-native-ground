Our Vision

A Living Repository of Rare Life

At present this property is a Cardamom estate. The complete focus was on Cardamom cultivation ignoring the possibilities of maintaining the biodiversity and looking at alternate possibilities of generating income from other sources.


The project aims to create a unique Biodiversity Sanctuary—a living repository of rare, endangered, and functionally significant plants, trees, insects, birds, and animals.

Core Philosophy

  • Coexistence, not control
  • Quality, not quantity

Alignment With

  • Climate resilience

  • Traditional ecological wisdom
The Land

Your 40–60 Acre Sanctuary

Strategically located adjacent to the 2024 disastrous landslide area, Native Ground is envisioned as a model for ecological resilience and restoration of Biodiversity.


At its highest point, a natural waterfall anchors the landscape, supported by abundant perennial water sources that enable efficient gravity-fed irrigation across the estate.

"Natural boulder-lined ponds offer serene bathing spots, while gentle streams along both boundaries enhance both the ecological integrity and the visual appeal of this unique property."

Location & Strategy

Situated on the Vellarimala Mountain slopes. Serving as an ecological restoration buffer adjacent to the landslide-affected area.

Natural Features

Featuring a beautiful waterfall, gravity irrigation, streams on boundaries, and natural boulder ponds.

Experience Biodiversity Live—Digitally

Native Ground is creating India’s first “Living Biodiversity Intelligence & Experience Platform”. Powering our mission through innovative revenue streams that sustain and secure its future.

  • Virtual Forest Experience: An immersive, always-on digital platform enabling global audiences to explore our flora, fauna, and ecosystem interdependencies.
  • Ecological Observatory: Advanced camera systems and field documentation capturing the lives of birds, mammals, insects, and plant systems.
  • Subscription Feeds: Full access to observatory feeds, data dashboards, and monthly expert sessions, creating predictable recurring income globally.
