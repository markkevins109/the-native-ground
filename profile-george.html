Director, Native Ground

George Cheriyan

Mr. George Cheriyan, with a Master’s degree in Production Engineering, brings a strong engineering perspective to his work. He has over four decades of diverse experience in engineering, project execution, and management. Over the course of his career, he has successfully led and delivered complex initiatives across domains, combining technical depth with strategic vision.

As the Founder and CEO of The Aviation Space Network and Machinist.in, Mr. Cheriyan has built influential platforms that connect professionals across the global manufacturing and aerospace ecosystem. His extensive international exposure—including engagement with leading companies across developed nations—has given him deep insight into global best practices and emerging trends.

At Native Ground, he brings not only his professional expertise and global network, but also a deep-rooted legacy and lifelong connection to the land. The Native Ground estates were originally developed with hard work and conviction by his father in the 1960s, and his own association with the land began in childhood—shaping his understanding of cultivation, ecology, and responsible stewardship from an early age.

Guided by an uncompromising commitment to nature and sustainability, he initiated Native Ground with a distinct purpose: to restore ecological balance, set new benchmarks in responsible land stewardship, and establish a model that will positively impact the health and lives of millions, both now and for generations to come.
